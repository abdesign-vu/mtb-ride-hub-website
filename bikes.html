Sram

A leading manufacturer of bicycle components and drives.

SRAM is a leading manufacturer of high-quality bicycle components, including derailleurs, cassettes, brakes and derailleurs. Founded in 1987, it was founded by Scott King, Sran Ray and Sam Patterson in Chichahu. The company has become a major player in cycling, known for its cutting edge technologically innovative products and commitment to excellence in the industry. Whether you're a serious racer or just enjoy trail riding, SRAM components are designed to help you get the most out of your ride. With features such as lightweight materials, precision engineering and intuitive operation, these components are built to perform at the highest level, regardless of your riding style. Some of the key benefits of using SRAM components include faster shifting, smoother braking and better handling. By choosing products from SRAM, you can improve the performance of your bike and take your riding to the next level. Plus, with a wide range of products to choose from, you can customize your bike to meet your specific needs and preferences.
